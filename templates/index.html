Process

How NEXUS Works

Three simple steps from roll number to mentor insight — powered by a trained Decision Tree model.

STEP 01
📝
Look Up Your Mentee
Enter the student's roll number. NEXUS fetches their details automatically, or lets you fill in the info if they're new.
→
STEP 02
🤖
AI Analyses Instantly
NEXUS computes a weighted composite score across 6 academic factors and classifies the student's performance risk level instantly.
→
STEP 03
📊
Get Actionable Results
Receive a risk level, predicted grade, visual charts, a mentor summary, and personalised recommendations — all saved to the database for future reference.
Output

Three Risk Levels

Every prediction is classified into one of three clear performance bands.

🏆
High Performance
Composite Score ≥ 72
Student is on track. Minimal intervention needed. Predicted Grade: A
📘
Medium Performance
Score 48 – 71
Monitor this student closely. Targeted support may improve outcomes. Predicted Grade: B
⚠️
At Risk
Score below 48
Immediate intervention required. Faculty should schedule counselling. Predicted Grade: C
