How NEXUS Works

Three simple steps from input to insight — powered by a trained Decision Tree model.

STEP 01
📝
Enter Student Data
Faculty fills in 6 academic metrics — attendance, study hours, internal marks, assignment score, mid-term score, and extracurricular index.
→
STEP 02
🤖
AI Analyses Instantly
NEXUS computes a weighted composite score and runs it through a Decision Tree classifier to determine the student's risk level.
→
STEP 03
📊
Get Actionable Results
Receive a risk classification, predicted grade, factor breakdown, and personalised recommendations — stored in the database for tracking.
Output

Three Risk Levels

Every prediction is classified into one of three clear performance bands.

🏆
High Performance
Composite Score ≥ 72
Student is on track. Minimal intervention needed. Predicted Grade: A
📘
Medium Performance
Score 48 – 71
Monitor this student closely. Targeted support may improve outcomes. Predicted Grade: B
⚠️
At Risk
Score below 48
Immediate intervention required. Faculty should schedule counselling. Predicted Grade: C
